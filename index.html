Projectidea

Overview

This project is a fitness app. After sports enthusiasts register and log in on the app, they can go in and choose the project they want to do, and improve their actions according to the video and text prompts in the app. On this app, sports enthusiasts can also choose a diet plan according to their needs, such as gaining muscle or reducing fat, and the app will give the details of the diet plan. In addition, fitness enthusiasts with a certain amount of experience can upload the certificate they have obtained and the city where they live in the app. After the certificate is eligible for background verification, these fitness enthusiasts can sell private lessons on the app. Those in need can communicate with these coaches directly on the app and purchase courses. After purchasing the course, the coach will go to the buyer’s gym to teach.

Motivation

Due to the impact of the epidemic, gyms are closed, more and more people stay at home to exercise, and the number of people using fitness apps has also increased sharply. Compared with last year, 64% of people spend more time on fitness apps (Koetsier, 2020). Between the first and second quarters of 2020, global health and fitness app downloads increased by 46% (Kamath, 2020). So it is very important to have a complete fitness app nowadays.

Description

Although there are a lot of fitness apps on the market, Their functions are not perfect. Such as the well-known fitness app KEEP in China, have a lot of home fitness teaching videos, but these functions are too introductory. Not all fitness enthusiasts are novices. Even those with a certain amount of experience need some more advanced plans. There should be two categories for advanced fitness groups: bodybuilding and powerlifting, many bodybuilding champions or powerlifting champions have shared their training plans on the Internet. These plans are very popular with everyone. These plans can be organized in the app. In the app, There will be a directory in the interface, when the user enters the app, they need to choose the category: home fitness, bodybuilding or powerlifting, and choose the level: beginner, medium or veteran. Based on these data, recommend some Training plan, When the user starts exercising, they only need to click the start button in the app to follow the plan, and the app can also help the user control the rest interval. In addition, these fitness apps ignore an important function, which is diet. There are three most important things in fitness: training, diet and rest. Without a good diet plan, people may be wasting their training. In terms of diet, there are also many bodybuilding champions sharing their daily diet plans on the Internet. Of course, Ordinary fitness enthusiast can’t copy the plans of these people because your training intensity and level are far less than others, but the daily protein intake of the trainer can be calculated. This app can roughly adjust the ratio of other nutrients in this diet plan according to the protein that user need. A more convenient point is that in this app, after choosing a meal plan, the cooking method of each meal will be given. Many people may give up after seeing the diet plan because they can't remember the cooking steps. The detailed steps are given in the app, which can make more people stick to it. Futhermore, Like the current home tutoring, fitness coaches can also provide “home service”. Because in China, the coaching level of some gyms is very poor. Strict backstage supervision on the app can guarantee the level of coaching. The fee for each personal training charged by the platform will include the fee for the coach’s visit to the buyer’s gym every time. Finally, some fitness products, such as protein powder and protective gear, will be sold in the app. The platform can buy fitness products in bulk from manufacturers to get a certain discount, so that users can buy them at a lower price when they are sold to users. The fitness products sold through the platform are also guaranteed for quality.
